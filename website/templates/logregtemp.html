Welcome to Sparkle Online Shop. A flask-based online shopping platform that enables users, or customers, to look for different products, offers, and online purchases. The project comprises of a list of products that are offered and are shown in different product categories. These products can be browsed by the user based on categories. The consumer has the option of adding a product to their shopping basket if they like it. When a person wants to check out, he must first register on the website. Next time, he can log in using the same ID and password. Now, he has the option of paying with a credit card, gcash, or cash upon delivery. Following a successful transaction, the user receives a copy of the shopping receipt on his email address. The complete system is made here using the Flask and Jinja frameworks.
